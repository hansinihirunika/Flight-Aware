Bürgenstock Resort

Located on the Bürgenberg, 450 m above Lake Lucerne in the canton of Nidwalden and close to Lucerne, Bürgenstock Hotels & Resorts - Palace Hotel offers accommodation in 109 classic yet modern rooms and suites. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.All rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV, the latest modern furnishings in terms of design, a seating area and private bathroom equipped with a bathtub or a shower. A terrace or balcony are featured in all rooms and guests will find bathrobes and slippers.The hotel features its own fine dining restaurant – Ritzcofier, its own lounge, Salon 1903 and a unique Museum corridor – a very high attraction featuring the iconic history of the Bürgenstock Resort.There are 9 other restaurants and bars located in walking distance to the resort which offer a wide range of cuisines from around the world.
