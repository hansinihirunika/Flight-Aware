Grand View Resort Beitou Hotel

As the only 5-star hotel in Beitou District, Grand View Resort is located in Beitou’s hot springs area, less than 10 minutes’ drive from both XinBeitou and Beitou Metro Stations. Uniquely designed by renowned architect Li Zuyuan (designer of Taipei 101), this resort features natural touches and classic tones, such as timber-framed interiors and natural cypress trees surrounded. It offers an outdoor pool and a spa.The Thermal Valley is 800 m away. It takes 40 minutes by MRT from Taipei 101.Guests can laze in the hot spring, or exercise at the fitness centre to start the day. Resort facilities include a 24-hour front desk. Concierge and laundry services are available.
