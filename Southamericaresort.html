Marulhos Suítes Resort

With a pool overlooking Muro Alto Beach, this resort offers a water park, tennis and soccer courts.Marulhos Suites e Resort is just a 10-minute walk from Cupe Beach, and 6 km from Porto de Galinhas City. Recife Airport is 60 km away.The Marulhos resort serves a daily buffet breakfast at the restaurant, which features a selection of fruits, breads and cold meats. Guests can have refreshing drinks at the luxurious bar.Boasting a balcony, the air-conditioned accommodations offer an LCD cable TV and minibar.While guests enjoy the facilities of this resort, children can have fun with the entertainment staff at the playground. A TV room and sauna are also available.
